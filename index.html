More Than Structures

Civil Engineer | Structural Design Engineer | Structural BIM Modeler

Licensed Civil Engineer practicing Structural Engineering Design since 2020, with experience in delivering practical and buildable solutions across residential to mid-rise building projects. My work focuses on bridging structural analysis and detailed documentation to ensure clarity, accuracy, and constructability in every project.

Proficient in Structural Drafting, having handled not only structural analysis and design but also the preparation of detailed drawings using both CAD and Revit. This integrated approach allows me to maintain control over the design process, ensuring that calculations, models, and drawings are well-coordinated and aligned with project requirements.

Dedicated to mastering Building Information Modeling (BIM) through self-directed learning, I specialize in Structural Modeling and Drafting within real project workflows. I am confident in my ability to produce accurate Revit models and detailed drawings, and I am committed to further refining my coordination skills and technical proficiency through professional guidance and collaborative experience.

With a strong foundation in Structural Engineering and a developing proficiency in BIM workflows, I aim to contribute to efficient, accurate, and well-coordinated design solutions while continuously advancing my technical capabilities.
